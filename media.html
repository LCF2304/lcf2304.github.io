BBQs Sounds

The following are common sounds we can listen while making BBQs. Let's taste it with the sound.

Fusion Bonfire
The bonfire sound of a BBQ, often referred to as the crackling or sizzling sound, is a distinctive auditory element associated with outdoor grilling and barbecuing. This sound is a result of various processes happening simultaneously during the cooking of food over an open flame or hot coals.
Fusion Cooking Oil
Brushing your BBQ items with cooking oil before grilling enhances moisture, prevents sticking, and creates a flavorful sear. Different oils add unique tastes, while ensuring even cooking and appealing grill marks.
Fusion Sizzler Bacon
BBQ sizzler bacon is a mouthwatering delight, bringing the irresistible smokiness of barbecue to crispy bacon strips. Perfectly grilled, it adds a savory and flavorful touch to any dish, elevating the classic bacon experience with the essence of outdoor cooking.
